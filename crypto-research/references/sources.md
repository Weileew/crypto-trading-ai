# Trusted Research Sources

Seed list for crypto trading research collection.

## Peer-reviewed and Preprint Repositories
- ArXiv: `https://arxiv.org/search/?query=cryptocurrency+trading&searchtype=all`
- SSRN: `https://ssrn.com/searchResults.cfm?query=cryptocurrency+strategy`
- ResearchGate topic searches on crypto market microstructure

## Institutional and Industry Research
- Coin Metrics Research Weekly
- Glassnode Academy and newsletter archives
- CryptoQuant Market Research
- Binance Research
- Kraken Intelligence

## Public Data Repositories
- Kaggle: crypto OHLCV datasets
- UCI Machine Learning Repository: crypto datasets
- CCXT unified exchange data library

## Naming / Structuring
When adding a source to `papers/`, use:
`YYYY - FirstAuthor - ShortTitle - Category.md`

Categories: `microstructure`, `sentiment`, `onchain`, `strategy`, `risk`

Fields to capture:
- URL
- Authors
- Year
- Summary
- Relevance to trading-advisor skill
- License / reuse note
